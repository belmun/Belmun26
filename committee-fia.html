fia (Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile)

Founded in 1904, the FIA emerged from the need to bring order and standardization to the rapidly evolving world of motorsport and automobility. What began as an effort to establish common regulations for early automobile competitions has evolved into the governing body that balances the competing passions of manufacturers, racing teams, drivers, and national sporting authorities across the globe. The FIA exemplifies unity in complexity by harmonizing technical innovation with safety imperatives, sustainability goals with sporting excellence, and commercial interests with the pure spirit of competition.
