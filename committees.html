Unity in Complexity

BELMUN 2026 offers nine unique committees spanning beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. Each committee presents delegates with complex global challenges requiring diplomacy, collaboration, and innovative thinking.

Beginner

Beginner Level Committees

WHO

World Health Organization

Address global health challenges and coordinate international responses to pandemics and health crises.

UNODC

UN Office on Drugs and Crime

Combat transnational organized crime, drug trafficking, and terrorism through international cooperation.

UNICEF

United Nations Children's Fund

Advocate for children's rights and ensure access to healthcare, education, and protection worldwide.
Intermediate

Intermediate Level Committees

FIFA

Fédération Internationale de Football Association

Navigate the governance of international football, balancing sporting integrity with commercial interests.

IPC

International Press Corps

Report on global events, hold power accountable, and navigate the challenges of modern journalism.

ICJ

International Court of Justice

Settle legal disputes between states and provide advisory opinions on international legal questions.
Advanced

Advanced Level Committees

USS

United States Senate

Engage in legislative debate and navigate the complexities of American democratic governance.

INTERPOL

International Criminal Police Organisation

Coordinate global law enforcement responses to transnational crime and security threats.

FIA

Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile

Balance innovation, safety, and competition in the governance of international motorsport.
