WHO (World Health Organization)

The World Health Organization is the directing and coordinating authority for health within the United Nations system. Delegates will address critical global health crises, pandemic preparedness, and healthcare accessibility. This committee will focus on developing comprehensive strategies for disease prevention, strengthening healthcare infrastructure in developing nations, and fostering international cooperation in medical emergencies.

Topics for discussion may include vaccine distribution equity, antimicrobial resistance, mental health initiatives, and the impact of climate change on global health outcomes. Delegates will work collaboratively to draft resolutions that promote universal health coverage and address health disparities worldwide.
