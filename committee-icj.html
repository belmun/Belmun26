icj (International Court of Justice)

Adjudicate complex international legal disputes using treaties, international law, and precedents to resolve conflicts between nations.

Delegates will engage in substantive debate on critical issues facing this committee, developing comprehensive solutions through diplomatic negotiation and collaborative problem-solving. This committee provides an excellent opportunity to develop leadership skills, enhance public speaking abilities, and gain deeper understanding of international relations.
